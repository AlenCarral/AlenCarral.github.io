About the Conference

Welcome to the official website of the Computational Imaging Conference 2025, themed Exploring Physical Limits: Innovations and the Future of Computational Imaging. This international conference brings together experts and researchers in the field of computational imaging to discuss breakthrough technologies, innovative methodologies, and future directions.

The conference will host approximately 100 participants from around the world. It will feature keynote speeches, technical sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities designed to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange in this rapidly evolving field.

Join us at the Tsinghua MADE Center in Milan, Italy, for this exclusive gathering of computational imaging specialists. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities in a stimulating environment perfect for scientific discourse and international collaboration.

Organizing Institutions

This conference is jointly organized by the following institutions:

  • Department of Electronic Engineering, Institute of Image and Graphics, Tsinghua University
  • Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge, United Kingdom
  • French National Centre for Scientific Research (CNRS), France
