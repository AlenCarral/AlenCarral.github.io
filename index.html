About the Conference

Welcome to the official website of the Computational Imaging Conference 2025, themed Exploring Physical Limits: Innovations and the Future of Computational Imaging. This international conference brings together experts and researchers in the field of computational imaging to discuss breakthrough technologies, innovative methodologies, and future directions.

The conference will host approximately 100 participants from around the world. It will feature plenary talks, keynote speeches, panel discussions, and networking opportunities designed to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange in this rapidly evolving field.

Join us at the Tsinghua MADE Center in Milan, Italy, for this exclusive gathering of computational imaging specialists. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities in a stimulating environment perfect for scientific discourse and international collaboration.

Organizing Committee

This premier international conference is jointly organized by three general co-chairs from three prestigious institutions at the forefront of computational imaging research. Their collaborative effort brings together diverse perspectives and expertise from Asia, Europe, and the global research community to advance the field of computational imaging.

  • Prof. Lu Fang, Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, China
  • Assoc. Prof. Qixiang Cheng, Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge, United Kingdom
  • Dr. Giuseppe Valenzise, French National Centre for Scientific Research (CNRS), France
  • Asst. Marco Marcon, Politecnico di Milano, Italy

For more information regarding the three general co-chairs and other committee members, please vist the Organizing Committee page.
