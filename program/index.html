Schedule Overview

The conference will begin on July 7, which is the registration day to welcoming participants from around the world.

The main program will take place over the following two days (July 8-9). Each half-day session will feature one plenary talk, two keynote speeches, and an engaging panel discussion with experts in the field.

The afternoon of the final day (July 9) will conclude with a comprehensive summary discussion of the conference highlights, followed by an exclusive guided tour of our cutting-edge laboratories.
